Pork Sinigang

The Pork Sinigang Sinigang is a Filipino soup or stew characterized by its sour and savoury taste. It is most often associated with tamarind, although it can use other sour fruits and leaves as the souring agent. It is one of the more popular dishes in Filipino cuisine.

Below are the list of ingrendients to cook this

  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • 1/2 kg pork (adobo cut)
  • sinigang mix
  • potatoes
  • cabbage
  • 2 cups of water
  • salt to taste
  1. put the water, onions, tomatoes in a pot then let it boil
  2. after it boils, put the pork, wait till it becomes tender
  3. the put the potatoes in
  4. put the sinigang mix and salt for the soup to have a taste
  5. lastly add the cabbage to finish the soup

Enjoy your hot and steaming pork sinigang soup
