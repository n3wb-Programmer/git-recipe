Adobo Recipe

Chicken Adobo

The Chicken Adobo. this recipe existed even before the Spanish empire colonized the Philippines. And this recipe is also present in different indigenous people living in the Philippines, all of them have different variations but they have things in common. they are marinated with soy sauce, uses pork or chicken, and has a lot of spices.

The recipes for this dish is listed below

  • 5 clovers of garlic
  • medium size onions
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 table spoons of vinegar
  • 1/2 kilo of chicken (your prefered part)
  • 3 bay leaves
  • table spoon of black pepper
  • salt and sugar to taste

The procedure on how to cook this is listed below

  1. in a bowl, marinate the chicken with soy sauce,garlic, and onions for an hour
  2. after marinating, place the marinated chicken with soy sauce in a pot. let it boil until the soy sauce has evaporated
  3. put the bay leaves and black pepper then stir
  4. pour the water in the pot
  5. wait till the meat is tender, then add the vinegar
  6. again, wait till it boils then add the spices

Now enjoy your adobo with friends or family!
