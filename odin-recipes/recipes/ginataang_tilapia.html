Ginataang Tilapia Recipe

Ginataang Tilapia

The Ginataang Tilapia is a creamy dish made from the milk of the coconut fruit it is a delicious meal that is popular in the Bicol Region where recipes made out of coconut are common

The recipe for this dish would be listed below

  • 2 cups of coconut milk
  • 1/3 kg of Tilapia
  • 4 clovers of garlic
  • 1 medium size onion
  • bundle of pechay (chinese cabbage)
  • 2 pieces of sili
  • 1 cup of water
  • ginger
  • salt and msg to taste
  1. in a pot, put the coconut milk, ginger, garlic, and onion. let it boil
  2. after it boils put the Tilapia and the stalk of the pechay
  3. after boil, put pechay leaves then add seasonings to taste
  4. lastly, add the sili

Now you have your ginataang tilapia, enjoy!
